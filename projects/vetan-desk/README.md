# Vetan Desk

Self-hosted staff management system designed for Indian educational institutions. Handles biometric attendance, leave management, payroll, shift scheduling, and organizational administration.

> Source code is maintained in a private repository. This page covers publicly distributed applications only.

## Overview

Vetan Desk (internally codenamed MIST -- Management and Intelligence for Staff Tracking) provides a complete staff operations platform:

- **Backend API** -- centralized business logic, RBAC, and data management (not publicly distributed)
- **Admin Dashboard** -- web-based administration console for HR and management (not publicly distributed)
- **Staff App** -- mobile application for individual staff members to manage their attendance, leave, and payslips
- **Lens Kiosk** -- tablet application deployed at physical entry points for face-recognition-based attendance

Only the Staff App and Lens Kiosk are distributed through this repository. The backend and dashboard are deployed directly by the organization.
